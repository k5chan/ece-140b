Product Interactions

Our MVP design is all about visual interpretation of data and easy app navigation.
The visual interpretation part of our product will be essentially a 3d model of
a skeleton going through the same range of motions of the user. This helps the Users
achieve their goal of getting better because they can overlay each attempt and see
the minute differences. This clearly would show how consistent/inconsistent a user is
in addition to showing good or bad patterns that the user is developing. App navigation
is also a huge part of our MVP design. With easy fillout tables to customize each device
to a specific user we can get through the hassle of asking for information about users
Also with the app we are leaning towards more of a social platform instead of a pure
goal based app making it something more reuseable. With the ability to see stats of
friends and family people can have friendly competitions making the product less
about self-improvement but also for it's social aspects.
