Our Product

What it is:
Our product will be a set of bands that strap to different parts of the body these bands would contain the sensors.(Instead we might have athletic apparel that have sensors integrated into them.)
Who it's for:
Our product is for new and amateur athletes who want to track their progress to help them improve their form
What it does:
Our product tracks the movement,angle of movement and acceleration of certain parts of the users body and compiles this information to approximate the location and trajectory of the packages.
How it works:
Our product works by using RF modules to wirelessly transmit data to a phone app. It is off this app that all the calcuations are made and where the user can receive input.
Key benefits:
Some key benefits of our product are that you can get real time feedback and our product would at a way lower price point compared to our competitors
