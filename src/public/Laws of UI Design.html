Laws of UI Design

This image shows the law of proximity. This shows the law of
proximity because in this screen shot you can clearly see how
Amazon blocks off certain categories with distance(proximity)
to clearly display relation i.e. All the movies are in the
products. same area and distanced from other

This image shows the Hick's law. This shows Hick's law
because it shows how the time it takes to make a decision
could be greatly impacted by the amount of options that
are present

This image shows Miller's law. This shows Miller's law
because the online menu is organized into chunks.
Chunks are easier to process and understand easier
because the average person and only keep 7 (+/-2)
items in working memory. The chunk contents are also in
now numbers.
