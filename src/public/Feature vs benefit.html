Features and their benefits

Low cost:
The low cost makes our product less committal relative to other options.
This benefits the essentially everyone but mainly the more common athlete.
This is because one can spend less and still get a great product without
making a dent in their pockets.
Wireless connection(wifi)
This feature heavily benefits customer experience because wires are
notoriously horrible to work with so minimizing the amount exposed to the
user would greatly improve the customer experience. The wifi of our product
makes it feel like the user isn't even wearing anything.
Uses Arduino D1 Mini
This feature overall greatly improves the customer experience because of
the Arduino D1 Mini's lightweight frame and it's corresponding sensors.
This benefits the more serious Athletes because practice with our product
and without would feel nearly identical.
Has interactive app
This features makes the user experience overall better. This is because
user can use the app like a social media platform for sports. Users can see
their friends profiles and feel interconnected in a thing that is bigger than
themselfs. This makes the app more than just an information gather but makes it
a social app.
Multi-sport/Multi functional
Because of the nature of our product we can not only use our product for
basketball but for other sports as well. This is beneficial to the common
athlete because the versility of our product makes our product less committal
as we have a general product instead of a one sport product.
