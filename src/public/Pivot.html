Our journey

A pivot we have already undertaken is we've changed hardware. Initially we wanted to use radio frequencies to transmit our data instead of bluetooth but we insteaded to opt for none of those options and just use wifi adapters so we can upload data straight to our databases. Another pivot we went through was determining our customer personas. We initially wanted to target amatuer athletes but we decided that a more accurate persona would be just a sports enthusiast. If we were to move on with this startup after ece 140b we would have to do a huge technology pivot because currently we're just not accurate enough with our sensors to collect reliable information.

Types of pivots we've taken

zoom-out pivot
We think that we preformed a zoom-out pivot because initially we decided to only have a product with a corresponding app but when we decided to add interconnectiveness in our app and make our app more like a social platform rather than just a data center for our product
customer segment pivot
Initially we thought our customers were amatuer athletes but we later realised more and more sports enthusaists would rather have our product
platform pivot
We initally started our app as an information feedback system for our product but it's turned into a psuedo social media platform.
technology pivot
We have had a technology pivot because we found out that radio frequencies were just too hard to work with so we switched to wifi arduino modules and subsequently went from 3 information transfers to just one.
